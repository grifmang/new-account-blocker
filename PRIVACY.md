# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 5, 2026*

## Data Collection

New Account Blocker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely within your browser.

## What the Extension Accesses

The extension reads publicly available account metadata (creation dates, verification status, follow status) from Twitter/X API responses that are already loaded by your browser. This data is used solely to determine which posts to filter and is held in temporary memory that clears when you close or reload the page.

## What is Stored Locally

The extension saves the following to your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local):

- Your preference settings (on/off toggle, age threshold, blue-check filter toggle)
- A list of usernames you have manually allowed
- A count of total filtered posts

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to any external server, third party, or the extension developer.

## Third-Party Services

The extension does not communicate with any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. It makes zero network requests.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
